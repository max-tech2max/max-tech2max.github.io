Week 2.3 Activities

Activity 1

How are <header> and <h1> related? What is the difference between them?

<header> defines regions of the page with header type information and and be located throughout as appropriate. <h1> thru <h6> are used to define and style individual headers as such that <h1> defines the most important heading and <h6> defines the least important heading.

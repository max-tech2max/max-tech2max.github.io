Sweet Corn Bread

This is my favorite corn bread recipe.

Ingredients

  • ¾ C Sugar
  • ½ C Salad Oil (Vegetable or Canola)
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 ½ C Flour
  • 3 tea. Baking Powder (1 tbsp)
  • 1/8 tea Salt
  • 1 ½ C Cornmeal
  • 1 C Milk

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 F
  2. Blend sugar and salad oil.
  3. Mix in eggs.
  4. Mix flour, baking powder, salt and corn meal together.
  5. Blend dry ingredients with the creamed mixture alternately with the milk.
  6. Poor into a greased, floured 9” sq. pan.
  7. Bake in a hot oven for 30 min. and a toothpick in center comes out clean.

Southwestern Chicken Soup

Simple and tasty soup, makes great leftovers.

Ingredients

  • 12oz can/jar Salsa Verde
  • 3 C cooked Chicken, shreaded
  • 15oz can Cannellini Beans, drained
  • 3 C Chicken Broth
  • 2 Green Onions, chopped
  • 1 Jalopeno, chopped
  • ½ C Sour Cream
  • Tortilla Chips

Steps

  1. Empty the salsa into a large saucepan. Add jalopeno and ½ green onions. Cook for 2 minutes over medium-high heat.
  2. Add chicken, beans, and broth. Bring to a boil, lower heat to a simmer, and cook for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Top each bowl with a sprinkling of onions, a dollop of sour cream, and some tortilla chips.
